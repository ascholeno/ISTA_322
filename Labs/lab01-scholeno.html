Two types of elements
There are two types of elemnts. Elements that have content have an opening tag and a closing. Empty elements do not have content. Empty elements have a closing tag but are ended by a closing bracket, which consists of </tagname>.

There are two types of elements. These are GENERIC elements. They are block level elements and inline elements. Block level elements are stacked vertically. Inline elements are "stacked" horizontally. Examples will follow.
